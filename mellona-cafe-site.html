Our Story

Founded in 2010, Mellona Café began as a small coffee cart in downtown Portland. What started as a passion project between two college friends quickly grew into the beloved community hub it is today.

We're committed to sourcing the highest quality beans directly from ethical growers around the world, then roasting them in small batches to ensure the freshest flavor in every cup.

Our name "Mellona" comes from the Roman goddess of honey, representing our sweet approach to hospitality and community.
